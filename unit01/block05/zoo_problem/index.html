Bears

Bears are omnivorous mammals that are found on nearly all continents.
Most bears are very large animals. In North America, the most common
bear is the Brown Bear.

Giraffes

Giraffes are herbivores found only in Africa. These spotted mammals are
unique in that they are very tall and have extremely long necks.

  • Frankie
  • Coconut

Lions

Lions are a species of big cats. They are found in Africa and hunt other mammals
found on the Serengeti. They are most commonly found in a grouping called a pride.

  • Mella
  • Karl

Monkeys

When someone thinks of monkeys they think of mammals that swing
from trees and eat fruit. These types are mostly found in the rainforest
regions of the globe. Many of these kinds of monkeys live in groups
and eat fruit from trees.

  • Cookie
  • Earl
  • Banana Pudding

Alligators

Alligators are reptiles that are found in fresh water swamps. Alligators
are unique in that they are only found in North America. Alligators can
grow to be very large and can move surprisingly fast on land.

  • Wren
  • Aspen
  • Mika
